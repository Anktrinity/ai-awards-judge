How I built an AI-assisted judging workflow for the Event Technology Awards, AI Edition 2026

Before scoring entries, I built the structure needed to judge consistently, fairly, and at scale across four criteria: Innovation, Impact, Execution, and Results.

What we built

  • One project folder
  • Intake + PDF extraction workflow
  • Master score sheet
  • Ranked results + feedback files

How we judged

  • Scored against the rubric first
  • Compared within each category
  • Calibrated for early-stage entries
  • Flagged weak or missing evidence

What is still manual

  • Final human review
  • Conflict check
  • Entering scores into evessio
  • Final submission in the judging portal
Step 1

Collect

Pull all entry files into one judging workspace.

Step 2

Extract

Convert PDFs into readable text for consistent review.

Step 3

Calibrate

Define how to score evidence, originality, and close calls.

Step 4

Score
