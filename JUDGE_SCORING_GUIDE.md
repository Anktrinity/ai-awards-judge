# Judge Scoring Guide

Use this exact framework for each entry.

## Criteria

### Innovation /100
How original and forward-thinking is the approach?
- Is AI applied in a new or distinctive way?
- Does it solve a real problem creatively rather than following the obvious path?
- Is there quality of thinking beyond off-the-shelf deployment?

### Impact /100
What difference has this made, or what difference is it credibly expected to make?
- For live solutions: engagement, time saved, revenue, cost reduction, satisfaction, adoption
- For pilots or beta: user testing, pilot outcomes, LOIs, grounded projections
- Reward specificity over vagueness

### Execution /100
How well has this been implemented or developed?
- Quality of build
- Robustness of delivery
- Change management or roadmap credibility
- Professionalism and realism at current stage

### Results /100
What has been achieved, and what does the evidence show?
- Client outcomes, retention, testimonials, recognition, hard metrics
- For early-stage: credible progress, pilot conversions, early user feedback, partnerships, investment
- Do not penalize for being early-stage, but keep evidence standards real

## Quick score bands
- 90 to 100: exceptional
- 80 to 89: strong
- 70 to 79: solid
- 60 to 69: mixed
- below 60: weak or under-evidenced

## Tie-break reminders
When scores are close, prefer:
1. clearer evidence
2. stronger category fit
3. more distinctive use of AI
4. more defensible real-world value
