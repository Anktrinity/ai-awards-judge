Questions / Answers
Explain how the AI works in your solution?
Uses an AI-powered automation layer to connect EventMobi with 1,000+ external platforms. Handles ﬁeld mapping, AI 
operations, duplicate detection, and allows for custom agentic workﬂows.
What speciﬁc problem does this AI solve?
Event teams waste hours keeping data in sync and still end up with out-of-date records when it matters. Integration 
Hub ﬁxes that, and goes further: it doesn't just sync data, it can act on it.
What makes your approach diﬀerent or innovative?
Most event platforms require custom development to connect external systems. Integration Hub not only oﬀers self-
service, no-code integration with 1,000+ platforms, it enables custom agentic AI workﬂows for true event automation.
VALIDATION
How did you test, validate, or quality-check the AI outputs to ensure they were accurate, safe, or reliable?
Integration Hub is built on a SOC2-certiﬁed automation infrastructure with a 99.999% uptime SLA. Each integration 
runs with automatic error detection, retry logic of up to ﬁve attempts, and detailed sync logs giving event teams full 
visibility into what moved, what failed, and why. Field mapping is validated against source and destination formats 
before the data moves. EventMobi monitors integration health across all active connections, with alerts triggered on 
any anomalous behaviour.
IMPACT
In one summary, explain the overall impact of your AI solution. Please cover four areas: innovation, execution, user or
customer outcomes, and measurable results.
Innovation
Connecting event tech stacks has always been limited to native integrations or can require a custom build, with weeks 
of lead time. Integration Hub changes that by embedding an AI-powered automation layer directly inside EventMobi. 
Event teams conﬁgure connections themselves in minutes using a no-code interface. The magic lies in the embedded 
workﬂow builder that can utilize ChatGPT, Claude or Gemini for AI operations on top of the data as it is being pulled 
from one system and written into another.
Execution
Integration Hub is built natively into EventMobi, so the experience is seamless. The catalog includes pre-built 
connectors for 1,000+ major platforms, CRMs, project management, and membership systems, but more can always 
be added. Setup follows a guided ﬂow: connect, map ﬁelds, run a test sync, go live. Syncing runs on conﬁgured 
intervals, with some platforms updating every minute. The execution can be a simple data sync or a custom agentic AI 
workﬂow.
Outcome
Event teams stop managing data, automate repetitive tasks with AI automation and get back to managing events. 
Last-minute registrations show up in check-in lists, and a text message can go out to notify VIP arrival. The event app 
can create attendee proﬁles automatically with a custom Linkedin and ChatGPT agentic workﬂow. For teams running 
multiple events a year, integrations conﬁgured once can carry forward, removing setup work entirely.

Results
Integration Hub removes the manual work that sits between event systems. Teams that previously managed data 
across multiple platforms report signiﬁcant reductions in pre-event reconciliation work. Connected badge scanning 
eliminates check-in bottlenecks. Gamiﬁcation workﬂows tied to session data drive measurable increases in attendee

CASE STUDY
Describe how the AI was used in practice.
The Meeting Space used EventMobi Integration Hub at their Stockholm forum to connect across multiple systems, 
including a bespoke CRM and a digital badge platform. Delegate data synced automatically, and badge scans at 
check-in and activity sessions fed directly into EventMobi, eliminating manual tick lists. At the close of the event, 
EventMobi automatically messaged attendees who had not returned their sustainable badges, replacing a previously 
manual tracking and outreach process.
What measurable results or improvements did it deliver?
(Include any evidence that shows real-world impact.) 
Badge scanning connected to EventMobi lets multiple staﬀ check in 100+ attendees simultaneously, replacing two-
person manual tick lists. Gamiﬁcation driven by badge-to-session integration drove 60%+ participation in welcome 
evening activities, up signiﬁcantly from the previous event without the real-time leaderboard. The automated badge 
return notiﬁcation eliminated a manual end-of-event process, freeing the team at the most time-critical point of the 
event, reducing their post-event stress.
MODEL TRANSPARENCY (optional)
What type of AI model or technique does your solution use? Please provide a simple, high-level explanation that
helps judges understand the approach (no conﬁdential or proprietary details required).
Integration Hub is powered by an AI-driven integration platform with over 1,000 pre-built connectors. The AI layer

on common data structures across CRM, registration, and membership systems. No large language model is involved. 
The system applies rule-based and ML-driven logic to match, merge, and route data between platforms reliably, 
without manual conﬁguration.
