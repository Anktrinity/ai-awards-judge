Description of your technology
Pointr’s AI MapScale™ is an AI-powered indoor mapping and routing engine that automatically creates, and optimizes 
navigation-ready digital maps for large, complex events and venues. Additionally, AI MapScale has a 95% accuracy 
when it detects map features. Beyond generating highly accurate indoor maps, AI MapScale intelligently creates the 
wayﬁnding routes that attendees use to move through a space, ensuring navigation reﬂects real-world movement 
patterns and operational constraints.
Using existing ﬂoor plans and spatial data, AI MapScale™ rapidly transforms venues into fully navigable digital 
environments. These maps and routes power turn-by-turn navigation across event apps, kiosks, and web experiences, 
helping attendees easily locate entrances, stages, seating areas, exhibitors, amenities, and services. By delivering clear 
routing and visual guidance, AI MapScale™ improves crowd ﬂow, reduces congestion, and minimizes frustration in 
busy, time-sensitive environments. 
For organizers, AI MapScale™ enables faster deployments, safer operations, and more engaging digital experiences 
by ensuring both maps and routes remain accurate as layouts change throughout an event. 
TECHNOLOGY DESCRIPTION
Explain how the AI works in your solution?
AI MapScale™ uses computer vision and machine learning to convert raw indoor ﬂoor plans, including CAD ﬁles and 
PDFs, into accurate, navigation-ready digital maps. The AI identiﬁes key structural elements such as walls, rooms, 
doors, corridors, vertical transitions, and points of interest, removes unnecessary detail, and builds a structured spatial 
graph of the venue. It then automatically generates optimized wayﬁnding routes by analyzing connectivity, accessibility, 
and movement constraints, dynamically updating as event layouts change. Large Language Models (LLMs) enable 
natural language, multilingual search and context-aware responses, allowing attendees to ask conversational questions 
and receive precise directions. Through multi-modal AI, the platform combines visual map data, routing intelligence, 
text inputs, event schedules, and real-time positioning to deliver personalized navigation and recommendations across 
mobile, web, kiosks, and digital signage—transforming static ﬂoorplans into intelligent, adaptive event experiences.
What speciﬁc problem does this AI solve?
Pointr's AI MapScale™ solves two critical challenges in indoor navigation: the manual creation of accurate indoor 
maps and the manual conﬁguration of reliable wayﬁnding routes. Traditional solutions require extensive human eﬀort to 
digitize maps and then manually deﬁne routes, making them slow, costly, and error-prone; especially for large or 
changing event venues.
AI MapScale™ automates both processes. It rapidly generates detailed maps from unstructured ﬂoor plans and 
intelligently creates navigation routes that reﬂect how people actually move through a space. This ensures attendees 
receive accurate, eﬃcient directions while organizers can deploy and maintain navigation experiences at scale without 
constant manual intervention.

Questions / Answers
What makes your approach diﬀerent or innovative?
AI MapScale™ is innovative because it combines AI indoor mapping with AI-driven route generation in a single spatial 
intelligence platform. Rather than stopping at visual map creation, it understands navigability: how spaces connect, 
which paths are accessible based on map content, and how users should be routed through complex environments.
This allows organizations to generate thousands of maps and their corresponding wayﬁnding routes in minutes, not 
weeks. By continuously keeping both maps and routes aligned with real-world changes and integrating natively with 
Pointr’s positioning platform, AI MapScale™ delivers scalable, reliable indoor navigation experiences uniquely suited to 
live events.
VALIDATION
How did you test, validate, or quality-check the AI outputs to ensure they were accurate, safe, or reliable?
AI MapScale™ was tested and validated using a data-driven evaluation framework centered on the F2 score, a metric 
that prioritizes recall while still accounting for precision—critical in navigation use cases where missing a wall, doorway, 
or route connection has greater impact than minor over-detection. AI MapScale™ was benchmarked against manually 
validated ground-truth maps across diverse venue types, including complex, multi-level event spaces. By measuring 
how accurately the system detected structural elements, points of interest, and navigable paths, and weighting results 
toward minimizing missed features, Pointr ensured the generated maps were both complete and reliable. Continuous 
training, cross-validation, and iterative quality checks improved model performance over time, with automated and 
human-in-the-loop reviews conﬁrming spatial accuracy, routing integrity, and real-world navigability before deployment. 
IMPACT
In one summary, explain the overall impact of your AI solution. Please cover four areas: innovation, execution, user or
customer outcomes, and measurable results.
Innovation
AI MapScale™ advances indoor navigation by unifying automated map creation with intelligent wayﬁnding route 
generation. By applying spatial AI to both understanding indoor environments and determining how people should 
move through them, it eliminates long-standing manual processes and enables dynamic navigation experiences at 
event scale. This dual capability unlocks fast, accurate, and continuously updated wayﬁnding in environments where 
layouts and traﬃc patterns frequently change.
Execution
AI MapScale™ is fully integrated into Pointr’s end-to-end navigation platform, automating the generation, validation, 
and updating of both maps and routes. As venues evolve, due to temporary closures, exhibitor changes, or 
reconﬁgured spaces; the AI updates routes without requiring full remapping. This enables rapid deployment and 
consistent execution across mobile apps, kiosks, and web-based experiences.
Outcome
For attendees, AI MapScale™ delivers clear, eﬃcient routes that reduce confusion and improve conﬁdence in 
navigating complex venues. Accurate routing minimizes backtracking and congestion, creating smoother journeys from 
entry to destination. For organizers, this results in fewer wayﬁnding inquiries, improved crowd ﬂow, and more eﬀective 
use of digital navigation channels.

CASE STUDY
Describe how the AI was used in practice.
At NRF 2025, AI MapScale™ was used to automatically generate both accurate indoor maps and optimized wayﬁnding 
routes across the 3.3 million-square-foot Javits Center. Existing architectural plans were ingested and transformed into 
navigation-ready digital environments, with AI identifying spatial elements, connectivity, and movement constraints.
These AI-generated maps and routes powered turn-by-turn navigation across the oﬃcial event app, on-site kiosks, and 
QR- and NFC-enabled access points. As exhibitor layouts and access paths evolved during the event, AI MapScale 
ensured routes remained accurate without manual reconﬁguration—supporting reliable navigation for tens of 
thousands of attendees throughout the show.
What measurable results or improvements did it deliver?
(Include any evidence that shows real-world impact.) 
Pointr’s technology played a pivotal role in elevating NRF 2026: Retail’s Big Show by dramatically improving how the 
more than 41,000 attendees and 1,000+ exhibitors experienced the sprawling 3.3 million-square-foot Javits Center. As 
the oﬃcial mapping and wayﬁnding partner, Pointr delivered interactive, AI-powered 2D and 3D maps that were 
seamlessly integrated into the NRF event app, on-site digital kiosks, and accessible app-free via QR codes and NFC 
tags, enabling attendees to eﬀortlessly ﬁnd booths, sessions, and amenities throughout the show. The impact was 
measurable: Pointr’s blue dot navigation experience supported over 28,000 individual navigation sessions, with users 
engaging with the maps for meaningful periods of time, underscoring the value of real-time wayﬁnding at large-scale 
events. Beyond helping visitors navigate eﬃciently, this technology elevated exhibitor visibility through customizable 
booth content and QR-driven engagement tools, and provided organizers with real-time analytics to monitor popular 
routes and points of interest, enabling dynamic improvements during the event.
