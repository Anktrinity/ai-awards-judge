Company proﬁle
EventMobi is event software that enables registration, check-in and badge printing, and event apps that drive 
engagement and networking. Teams can run complex events without juggling multiple tools.
YOUR PRODUCT
Description of your technology
MobiAI Registration Concierge is an AI assistant built right into the conference website registration experience. It 
answers questions during and after registration based on the event’s sessions, pricing, speakers, and logistics. It 
responds diﬀerently depending on who’s asking, so exhibitors or speakers get diﬀerent info than attendees. Questions 
are answered in seconds, on the same page where registration happens, helping them toward the next step.
TECHNOLOGY DESCRIPTION
Explain how the AI works in your solution?
Uses a large language model trained on each event’s data: sessions, FAQs, pricing, logistics. Reads natural-language 
questions and returns context-speciﬁc answers by attendee type in under 3 seconds.

Questions / Answers
What speciﬁc problem does this AI solve?
Attendees, speakers, and exhibitors abandon registration when they can't ﬁnd answers, costing organizers 
registrations and ﬁlling support inboxes with repeat questions, even after registration.
What makes your approach diﬀerent or innovative?
Built into EventMobi's platform, not added on top. Trained on real event content and responsive to attendee context. 
The ﬁrst to embed contextual AI as a website widget across the full event journey.
VALIDATION
How did you test, validate, or quality-check the AI outputs to ensure they were accurate, safe, or reliable?
Registration Concierge is in active beta across live events. EventMobi is running a structured impact study across 50+ 
conferences to assess accuracy, attendee experience, and conversion data. Internal testing conﬁrmed the system 
accurately surfaces sessions, FAQs, and logistics in under 3 seconds. Responses are validated against organizer-
approved source content in EventMobi. The AI answers only from what the organizer has conﬁgured, keeping every 
response accurate and on-brand.
IMPACT
In one summary, explain the overall impact of your AI solution. Please cover four areas: innovation, execution, user or
customer outcomes, and measurable results.
Innovation
The registration ﬂow has been a form, an FAQ page, and an email address since the beginning of online registration. 
MobiAI Registration Concierge replaces the FAQ page with something that actually talks back. It knows the diﬀerence 
between what a speaker needs and what a sponsor needs. It can help an attendee draft a business case for attending, 
with their manager’s name in it, written in their voice. The interaction becomes genuinely useful without adding any 
work for the event team.
Execution
Registration Concierge is native to EventMobi’s registration product. Setup uses content organizers already have in 
their account, with no separate tool to conﬁgure. The AI is trained from that content and deployed on the registration 
page as part of the existing workﬂow. Currently in beta, the product is being tested and reﬁned through a 50+ 
conference study, with interaction rates, response accuracy, and conversion data shaping every development decision.
Outcome
Attendees get direct answers to direct questions in under 3 seconds, without leaving the page. Support teams ﬁeld 
fewer repetitive questions. Internal research predicts that 40-60% of registration website traﬃc will include a concierge 
interaction. EventMobi projects a 10-25% reduction in bounce rate and a 30-90 second increase in time on page. That 
extra time is the window in which an undecided visitor becomes a registrant.
Results
In beta, Registration Concierge answers questions in under 3 seconds. EventMobi projects that more than 3 in 4 
visitors will engage with the concierge, with a 10-25% anticipated reduction in bounce rate and a 10-25% increase in 
unique visitor conversion rate. A 50+ conference study is underway to validate these numbers across a range of event 
sizes and formats. Full results will be published once the beta program concludes.

Describe how the AI was used in practice.
In beta testing, a professional association conference registration site was set up representing multiple attendee types. 
Each received relevant answers without being redirected to a FAQ page or waiting on an email reply. One test attendee 
used the concierge to generate a draft justiﬁcation email to their manager, personalized with their name and speciﬁc

What measurable results or improvements did it deliver?
(Include any evidence that shows real-world impact.) 
Attendees received accurate answers in under 3 seconds, compared to minutes of independent searching or waiting 
on support replies. Internal research projects a 10-25% reduction in bounce rate and a 10-25% increase in unique 
visitor conversion rate for registration pages using the concierge. EventMobi’s 50+ conference study is collecting data 
to validate these projections across event types, sectors, and audience scales, with results expected after the beta 
program completes.
MODEL TRANSPARENCY (optional)
What type of AI model or technique does your solution use? Please provide a simple, high-level explanation that
helps judges understand the approach (no conﬁdential or proprietary details required).
MobiAI Registration Concierge is built on an LLM (currently Anthropic Claude, though easily changed) using retrieval-
augmented generation (RAG). It retrieves answers from a knowledge base built from each organizer's event content 
rather than general training data. This grounds responses in veriﬁed, event-speciﬁc information and prevents the model 
from answering outside its scope. The result is an AI that behaves predictably and only answers from what the 
organizer has approved.
